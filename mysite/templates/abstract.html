ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

Submission of abstract for the 5 th ASA conference is invited for both oral and poster presentations. Before doing so, please carefully read all of the guidelines for a successful abstract submission. The organizers reserved the authority to accept or reject submission that do not meet with all of our guidelines. Revision of delegate abstract may be required based on decision of the organizing committee.

Abstract submission example file can be download here

Guideline for oral presentation

  • Abstract must be submitted online by 31st October 2018 Thailand time
  • Abstract should be written in English
  • Abstract cannot contain special symbols, diagrams, photographs or tables
  • The abstract title should not exceed 20 words
  • The abstract text should not exceed 2,000 characters (including space)
  • Oral presentation is limited to 15 minute
  • All presenters MUST register for the conference

    • Guideline for poster presentation

  • Abstract must be submitted online by 31st October 2018 Thailand time
  • Abstract should be written in English
  • The abstract title should not exceed 20 words
  • The abstract text should not exceed 2,000 characters (including space)
  • Language used in poster presentation is English
  • The size of the poster is A0 (84.1 x 118.9 cm)
  • All presenters MUST register for the conference
